01

Born from
obsession.

Seven years. Forty-three prototypes. One uncompromising vision. The AURENIX NOVA X1 began as a whisper — a belief that no headphone had ever truly captured what live music feels like.

Our founders sat in concert halls, recording studios, and mountain silences. They listened. Not to headphones. To the world. Then they built something worthy of it.

7 Years of Development
02

Aerospace
precision.

The headband is machined from Grade 5 titanium — lighter than aluminum, stronger than steel. The ear cups are carved from a single billet of anodized aluminum, achieving tolerances tighter than 0.02mm.

The cushions? Full-grain Nappa leather sourced from a 200-year-old tannery in Córdoba, Spain. Temperature-regulating memory foam beneath. Every contact point reimagined.

Grade 5 Titanium Anodized Aluminum Nappa Leather
03

Sound that
breathes.

At the core of NOVA X1 are our proprietary 50mm Beryllium-coated dynamic drivers — the same material used in concert hall tweeters and professional studio monitors.

Ultra-light, ultra-rigid. Beryllium moves 40% faster than conventional drivers, allowing transients to arrive with surgical precision and fade with natural, unhurried decay.

4Hz → 96kHz Frequency Response
04

Intelligence
that listens.

The AURENIX AX-7 Neural Processor runs our proprietary Adaptive Intelligence Engine — a real-time AI model that maps your ear canal acoustics in under 2 seconds and continuously tunes the sound signature to your unique anatomy.

Six outward-facing microphones. Four inward-facing. The system analyzes 65,536 acoustic data points per second to deliver ANC that is not just reactive — it is predictive.

05

An object
for eternity.

Every NOVA X1 is assembled by hand in our atelier in Kyoto, Japan. A team of twelve master craftspeople. Each pair takes 14 hours to complete. Each is signed and numbered.

This is not a product you replace in two years. This is an heirloom. A statement. A companion for life.
